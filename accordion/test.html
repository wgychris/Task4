Overview

Nothing's stronger, longer lasting, better on your tough aches and pains than Advil. And nothing works on more pains than Advil. In fact, to treat their own aches and pains, the medicine doctors use most is the medicine in Advil.

Ingredients

Active Ingredient (in each Tablet): Ibuprofen (200 mg). Inactive Ingredients: Acetylated Monoglycerides, Beeswax and/or Carnauba Wax, Croscarmellose Sodium, Iron Oxides, Lecithin, Methylparaben, Microcrystalline Cellulose, Pharmaceutical Glaze, Povidone, Propylparaben, Silicon Dioxide, Simethicone.

Directions

Do not take more than directed. The smallest effective dose should be used. Do not take longer than 10 days, unless directed by a doctor (see Warning). Adults and Children 12 Years and Over: Take 1 tablet every other 4 to 6 hours while symptoms persist. If pain or fever does not respond to 1 tablet, 2 tablets may be used.

Warning

Allergy Alert: Ibuprofen may cause a severe allergic reaction especially in people allergic to aspirin. Symptoms may include: hives; shock; facial swelling; skin reddening; asthma (wheezing); rash; blisters. In an allergic reaction occurs, stop use and seek medical help right away. Stomach Bleeding Warning.
